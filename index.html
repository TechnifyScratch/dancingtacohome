FAQ

Quick answers for first-time visitors.

Is there any backend?

Not right now. This landing page is static and designed to be hosted on Vercel with no backend required.

Can this support paid plans later?

Yes — the site is already structured with pricing tiers. When you’re ready, you can add payments (Stripe, etc.) and a real “Pro” experience.

Where do I link users to install?

Use the “Add to Chrome” buttons — they point directly to your Chrome Web Store listing.

Do I need a cookie banner?

If you don’t add analytics or tracking cookies, typically no. If you do, update the privacy policy and comply with relevant laws.
